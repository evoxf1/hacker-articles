Macro headlines are now a first-order market variable. Daily reporting that ignores policy cadence is incomplete.

## What I am watching

- Rate commentary from central banks.
- Export controls that alter supply chains overnight.
- Regulatory pressure on AI and cloud concentration.

## Brutal framing

If you are still writing market updates as if this is only an earnings story, you are late.

Readers need:

- What changed in policy.
- Which sectors are directly exposed.
- What to monitor over the next seven days.
