The AI world shipped hard again this week, but users are still left guessing what data is used, how outputs are ranked, and what lock-in costs look like after month one.

## What changed today

- Vendors pushed lower-latency model variants and aggressive free credits.
- Enterprise features got better dashboards, but still weak portability.
- More products now hide prompts and ranking controls behind UI gloss.

## Brutally honest take

Speed is not the problem anymore. Trust is.

If you are building a business on one model provider, ask:

1. What happens to cost after free credits?
2. Can you export data and prompts cleanly?
3. Is there a fallback path when quality drops?

Teams that answer those three now will move faster later.
